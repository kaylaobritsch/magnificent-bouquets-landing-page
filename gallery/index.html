Stately houses, Castles & Cathedrals

Flowers at an event should have a sense of balance and rhythm. A natural flow and harmony connecting all the arrangements, making you want to look and keep on looking. And every bride should savour that spectacular moment, with a grand entrance carrying her Magnificent Bouquet. It’s about creating a series of WOW moments to surprise and delight guests as they move through your venue – whether it’s a castle, church, hotel, marquee, stately house or favourite restaurant.

Funeral and Natural Burial Flowers

At Magnificent Bouquets we believe in sympathy with empathy. Funerals are your last chance to say goodbye. Having lost three dear ones within six months of each other, we can truly appreciate how awful it is to experience that loss. And every sympathy tribute that is created has those feelings deeply embedded in, allowing us to produce arrangements that really say what you feel in a beautiful way.
